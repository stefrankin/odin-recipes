Beef Bulgogi

Description

This restaurant-worthy beef bulgogi recipe is easy to make and impossible to resist. A sweet and savory marinade, made with ingredients you probably already have on hand, ensures melt-in-your-mouth bulgogi every time.

Bulgogi, or Korean barbecue, literally means "fire meat." Thin slices of beef (or sometimes pork) are marinated in a sweet-savory sauce, then grilled to juicy and flavorful perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2.5 tablespoons white sugar
  • .25 cup chopped green onion
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • .5 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients
  2. Step 2: Whisk soy sauce, green onion, sugar, garlic, sesame seeds, sesame oil, and pepper together in a bowl.
  3. Step 3: Place flank steak slices in a shallow dish. Pour marinade over top. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  4. Step 4: Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  5. Step 5: Quickly grill flank steak slices on the preheated grill until slightly charred and cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  6. Step 6: Serve hot and enjoy!
